# Code of Conduct

We follow the [Contributor Covenant v2.1](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/).

## Our pledge
We pledge to make participation in the DevSwap community a harassment‑free experience for everyone,
regardless of background or identity, and to act in ways that contribute to an open, welcoming,
diverse, and healthy community.

## Standards
Examples of positive behavior: empathy and kindness, respecting differing opinions, giving and
gracefully accepting constructive feedback, focusing on what is best for the community.

Unacceptable behavior: harassment, trolling, insulting comments, publishing others' private
information, or other conduct reasonably considered inappropriate.

## Enforcement
Report unacceptable behavior to **conduct@devswap.pro**. Maintainers will review and respond, and may
remove, edit, or reject contributions, or ban contributors, for behavior that violates this code.
